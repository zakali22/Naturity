Hair Oil

This hair oil is designed to grow every inch of your hair fibres. Each property and ingredient is 100% natural and so can guarantee a more healthier solution to hair maintenance.

£20

How to use
Warm a generous amount between the fingertips and gently massage onto the face, neck and décolletage, avoiding the eye area. Reapply every 2 hours or after towel drying, swimming or vigorous exercise. Do not use on damaged or broken skin. Avoid prolonged exposure to the sun. Wear protective clothing, hats and eyewear.
Ingredients
Aqua (Water), Glycerin, Glyceryl Stearate Citrate, Heptyl Undecylenate, Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride, Cocoglycerides, Citrus unshiu Peel Extract, SD Alcohol 40-A (Alcohol Denat.), Ascorbyl Glucoside, Althaea officinalis Root Extract, Chamomilla recutita (Matricaria) Flower Extract, Rosa gallica Flower Extract, Glycyrrhiza glabra (Licorice) Root Extract
